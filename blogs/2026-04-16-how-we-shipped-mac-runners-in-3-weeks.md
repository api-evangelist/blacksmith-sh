Blacksmith shipped Mac runners in three weeks using "closed-loop development" with AI agents that could self-verify work across four iterative phases: VM booting, filesystem setup, fleet deployment, and end-to-end testing. The approach minimized human context-switching compared to their three-month Windows runner development cycle.
