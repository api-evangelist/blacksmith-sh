GitHub is implementing a $0.002-per-minute platform fee for all Actions usage starting March 1, 2026, eliminating the previously free control plane. The post explains that self-hosting is no longer cost-free and recommends optimizing CI performance to minimize billable minutes through faster infrastructure and caching strategies.
